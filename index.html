Client Project Website Redesign

Wax Thread & Scissors

Wax Thread & Scissors is a full stack salon that offers many different services. I have been tasked in redesigning their website bringing them more exposure in the beauty industry.

Non Profit Website Redesign

Womens Club of Sugarloaf Country Club

The WCSCC is a nonprofit organization that strives to help different charities within Duluth County. Our team was tasked in redesigning their logo and website making it more appealing and attractive to the residents of Sugarloaf Country Club.

Government Website Redesign

Department of Treasury

As we all know, government agency websites are a pain to deal with. Our team was tasked in choosing a government agency in order to redesign their entire website including navigation, content, and structure. We developed a new Style Guide for all our designs.

Student Information System

BLOOM Works App

For this project, we have been tasked with finding an issue in the world, and building an app in order to help rectify that issue. We chose the issue of there being a lack of a Student Information System in grades K - 8. Our goal was to help parents, teachers, and faculty communicate effectively.

Travel Planning App

Plan4U

This was my first project as a UX Designer. My task was to develop an app that will help users while planning their trip. I developed an app that would build future customers itinerary for them complete with flight, accommodations, and site seeing suggestions.
